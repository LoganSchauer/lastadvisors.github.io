FAQ

Questions we hear all the time

How is this different from hiring a full-time CFO or COO?

You get senior-level expertise without the $200K+ salary, benefits, and ramp-up time. We plug in fast, deliver results, and scale our involvement up or down as your needs change.

What size companies do you work with?

Mostly companies between $2M and $50M in revenue. Big enough to have real complexity, small enough that every dollar and decision matters.

How long does a typical engagement last?

Project-based work usually runs 4 to 12 weeks. Fractional roles are ongoing, typically 3+ months. We don't do long-term contracts for the sake of it — we stay as long as we're useful.

Do you work with companies outside the US?

Yes. We've worked with teams across multiple time zones. Most of our work is remote-first.

What does pricing look like?

It depends on scope. Project engagements are fixed-fee. Fractional roles are monthly retainers. We'll give you a clear number after our discovery call — no surprises.

Ready to stop guessing and start building?

Book a 30-minute discovery call. No pitch, no pressure — just a real conversation about where you are and where you want to go.
